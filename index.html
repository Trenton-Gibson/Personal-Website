Carbone's Pizzeria Marketing System

I'm building a Marketing System for the Carbone's Pizzeria restaurant chain. There are over 30 locations distributed across the states of Minnesota, Wisconsin, and Montana. The system would identify newly sold homes around the area of a particular Carbone's location. With this information, Carbone's locations could send marketing material in the mail to those newly sold homes. This would create a specific target for marketing allowing for better use of marketing dollars. To view the code for this project click on the link that says "My Coding Projects".
